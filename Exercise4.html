Molten Lava Cake

These Molten Chocolate Lava Cakes are the perfect small 
batch dessert.Molten chocolate cake is a popular dessert that combines 
the elements of a chocolate cake and a soufflé. Its name derives from 
the dessert's liquid chocolate center, and it is also known as chocolate 
moelleux, chocolate lava cake, or simply lava cake. These lava cakes are incredibly easy to make and ready
in less than 30 minutes.This can literally melt-in-your-mouth-perfection.
So delicious. its easy to make. Let me know if you agree!
Ingridients Needed
    

  • 1 stick (4 ounces) unsalted butter
    •        

  • 6 ounces bittersweet chocolate, preferably Valrhona
    • 

  • 2 eggs
    • 

  • 1/4 cup sugar
    • 

  • 2 egg yolks
    • 

  • Pinch of salt
    •     

  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
    •

Step and Preparation on Cooking

  1. Chop 6oz of semisweet chocolate then transfer it and the butter to a bowl then heat.
    2. 

  2. Stir until it’s smooth.
    3. 

  3. Whisk 2 eggs and 2 yolks with a pinch of salt, then add 1/4 cup of sugar and mix in a stand mixer until a light yellow color and thicker in texture
    4. 

  4. Fold in the chocolate.
    5. 

  5. Add flour and mix in.
    6. 

  6. Transfer the batter to buttered and floured ramekins.
    7. 

  7. Bake at 450 degrees for about 6-8 minutes.
    8. 

  8. Let them sit for a minute before transfer them to a dessert plate.
    9. 

  9. Sift with powdered sugar then enjoy!
