Chicken Adobo

The famous Chicken Adobo originated in the Philippines. The dish is prepared using the 
Inadobo style of cooking. It means cooking meat or seafood with vinegar and mostly soy sauce. 
It is a popular method during the olden days when refrigerators and freezers 
were not yet available because vinegar helps extend the shelf life of food.
Another popular variation is pork adobo using pork belly.
